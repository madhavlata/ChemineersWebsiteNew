ABOUT US

What we do?

Our society continuously organizes several activities to promote cultural interactions and overall development of our department's students.

How we do it?

The society conducts various departmental events. These events may include the Happy Hours, Technical workshops, Freshers Party, Alumni Talks, Farewell Party, Sports tournaments, etc

What we achieve?

We help students identify campus resources and foster harmonious relationships among the students, faculty, and alumni of the CHE department and ensure that there is no communication gap between them

EVENTS

Farewell and Freshers

The society organizes a wide range of activities for the benefit of the Chemical department community at IITK. These activities range from fun-filled Happy-Hours to large Symposiums.

Happy Hours

Happy Hours are organized by the Chemineers Society which serve as a means to boost the interaction of students with professors as well as a means to get to know more seniors.

Simutech Projects and Blogs

Simutech Projects and Blogs

Projects are offered by the Mentors of Chemineers Society to enhance the skills and prepare students for incoming intership season.

Exergy

Exergy is a pan-India Chemical Engineering Fest organized by the Chemineers Society, IIT Kanpur. This fest includes workshops, poster presentations and talks from renowed chemical engineers.
